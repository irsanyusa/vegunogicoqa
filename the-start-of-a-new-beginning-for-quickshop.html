The start of a new beginning for Quickshop

Publish date: 2024-09-02

To celebrate this feat, It is offering a zero signup and processing fee to merchants who sign up on the platform and a 20% discount on the first three orders for buyers

With over 200+ verified vendors and counting, this new version aims to elevate the online shopping experience in Nigeria and Africa.

What’s New On Quickshop?

ADVERTISEMENT

The new Quickshop app is designed to connect online shoppers with verified vendors more efficiently and securely. The latest features provide vendors and shoppers with unprecedented flexibility and convenience.

Key Features and Benefits:

For Vendors:

  • Zero Processing Fees: Vendors keep 100% of their sales revenue, maximising their profits.
  • Discount/Coupon Creation: Vendors can now create discounts and offer coupons for their products, helping them attract more customers.
  • Analytics Dashboard: The new analytics dashboard provides detailed insights for proper financial and inventory management, enabling vendors to manage their businesses more effectively.
  • Business Loan: Businesses on Quickshop can apply for business loan to help boost their business

ADVERTISEMENT

For Shoppers:

  • Verified Vendors: Shop with confidence, knowing that every vendor on Quickshop is verified.
  • Delivery Options: Shoppers can choose their preferred delivery options or opt for in-store pickup, offering flexibility and convenience.
  • First Purchase Discount: Enjoy a discount on delivery fees for the first three purchases on the platform.
  • Referral earnings: Shoppers earn by referring their friends and family to shop on Quckshop, the referral earnings can then be used for the purchase on the platform.

Speaking about the new app update, Quickshop Product Marketing Manager, Dawood Kehinde noted, "With this release, we demonstrate our commitment to helping small businesses, vendors, and physical store owners to broaden their sales and improve their businesses by connecting them with online shoppers who are looking for convenience, competitive price and peace of mind when it comes to online shopping."

This is in line with our commitment to providing excellent online shopping experience to both sellers and buyers on Quickshop, he restated.

He also highlighted the Quickshop business community created to help small business owners have a kind of support group where they can network and learn from each other and other successful business owners through interactive sessions held monthly
