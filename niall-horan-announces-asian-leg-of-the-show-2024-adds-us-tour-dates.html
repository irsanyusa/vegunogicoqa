Niall Horan Announces Asian Leg of The Show 2024, Adds US Tour Dates

Publish date: 2024-06-13

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Former One Direction band member Niall Horan is bringing his The Show Live on Tour to Asia next year. The singer also announced that he’s added more US dates for his concert’s North American leg.

Niall Horan Set to Travel Around Asia for The Show 2024

On October 12, Horan took to social media to share that he’ll be making stops in Asia for his upcoming tour. As per the announcement, Asian fans will be able to see him perform four concerts in May 2024.

“I’m bringing The Show Live On Tour over to Asia next year ! The Tokyo date is on sale now and tickets for Singapore, Jakarta and Manila go on sale 18 Oct . Sign up for my newsletter at niallhoran.com for access to Monday’s presale,” he wrote.

The Voice coach will begin the Asian leg of the tour on May 9 in Singapore at the Indoor Stadium on May 9. He will then go to Jakarta, Indonesia to perform at Beach City International Stadium on May 11.

On May 13, the singer will head to Manila, Philippines at the SM Mall of Asia Arena. Finally, he will conclude his Asian tour in Tokyo, Japan on May 15 at the Garden Theatre.

Upon seeing his announcement, his fans went hysterical out of excitement. Asian fans clearly can’t wait to witness to see Horan live.

At present, Horan’s Japan concert show tickets are on sale. The remaining shows of his Asian leg will be available for purchase on October 18. For pre-sale tickets, fans must wait until October 16, Monday.

He Adds More US Tour Dates

Short after his Asian tour news, Horan revealed that he’s adding more US tour dates for the North American leg of The Show. According to Capitol Records, they decided to attach more dates on the schedule “due to incredible demand.”

“I’m absolutely thrilled to share that I’ve added even more US dates to The Show Live On Tour ! This includes a second date at Madison Square Garden , one of my all time favourite venues in the world. I cannot believe the first date there sold out so quickly so had to add a second night. Tickets go on sale Oct 18,” Horan wrote.

Additional dates include June 1 at Ameris Bank Amphitheatre in Alpharetta, Georgia; June 5 at PNC Music Pavilion in Charlotte, North Carolina; June 13 at Madison Square Garden in New York, New York; July 13 at Ruoff Music Center in Noblesville, Indiana; August 2 at Dos Equis Pavilion in Dallas, Texas; and August 3 at Moody Center in Austin, Texas.

“Mexico and South America , don’t worry there will be shows coming your way soon,” he wrote.

The North American leg of The Show Live on Tour will launch on May 29, 2024 at Hard Rock Live in Hollywood. The same will include shows at Nashville’s Bridgestone Arena (June 3), New York City’s Madison Square Garden (June 13 & June 14) and The Kia Forum in Inglewood, CA (July 27 & 28).
