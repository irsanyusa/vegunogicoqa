Selena Gomez, Benny Blanco Wrap Up The 2024 Golden Globes with a Hot Kiss

Selena Gomez and Benny Blanco are now displaying more of their romance on social media. The “Who Says” hitmaker and her music producer boyfriend shared an intimate kiss during the 81st annual Golden Globes on January 7.

Selena Gomez, Benny Blanco Share Intimate Kiss at 2024 Golden Globes

Following the 2024 Golden Globes, the actress shared a photo of her kissing Blanco on her Instagram Story. Gomez held her boyfriend on the neck while stunning in her red and black Giorgio Armani dress and black jacket.

“I won,” she wrote as caption.

On Instagram, the Only Murders In the Building star also shared pictures of them together from the awards night. In the photos, the two can be seen being all lovey-dovey backstage.

Gomez and Blanco have known each other for quite some time. The two even worked together for the Spanish song “I Can’t Get Enough.”

The pop singer confirmed her romance with Blanco in early December after a fan account posted a black-and-white image of her and the producer together. In the comment section, the singer replied to a comment by fans.

“He is my absolute everything in my heart,” she wrote.

She also responded to a comment, saying, “Then why has he been the best thing that’s ever happened to me. The end.”

On Wednesday, the couple were seen seizing a date night at Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles. Gomez and Blanco were sitting courtside as they watch a game between the L.A. Lakers and the Miami Heat. The singer and her beau didn’t even try to hide their romance, cuddling close and holding hands.

She is “Super Happy and Present” Amid New Romance

It’s no secret that Gomez struggled with body image and self-confidence. Yet, amid her romance with the record producer, Gomez is reportedly “super happy and present.”

“She seems to be really feeling herself at the moment,” the source told People. “Maybe that’s Benny or maybe that’s just where she’s at personally and professionally.”

The pop singer’s happiness has transcended even to her circle. Basically, “everyone is so happy that she’s happy.” Be that as it may, Gomez’s friends are keen about her and Blanco’s romance.

“Everyone was a fan of hers before Only Murders [in the Building], and now everyone is protective of her. With her cast and crew, the feeling is ‘any boyfriend of Selena’s is a boyfriend of ours,'” the insider said.
