It’s almost time for another America’s Got Talent live results show for Season 18. Eleven acts took the stage on Tuesday night, but only two of them can move on to the finale thanks to America’s vote. Wednesday’s results show will also feature a performance from AGT winner Shin Lim.

Shin Lim to Perform During AGT Results Show

During the Qualifiers 2 results show on Wednesday night, AGT Season 13 winner Shin Lim will be taking the stage as a guest performer. Host Terry Crews announced the exciting news during Tuesday night’s performance show.

In case you don’t know, Lim is a magician who wowed Got Talent viewers with his talent for sleight of hand in 2018. Despite not earning a Golden Buzzer, Lim went on to win the entire season thanks to America’s votes.

The next year, Lim returned to compete on the first season of AGT: The Champions. He ended up winning that competition as well. Lim has returned several times as a guest performer. We can’t wait to see what the magician has up his sleeve for his latest performance.

Which Acts Will Go Through to the Finale?

On Tuesday night, 11 more acts took the AGT stage for a chance to earn America’s vote and go through to the Finals. Those acts were Ahren Belisle, Alfie Andrew, Andrew Stanton, Atlanta Drum Academy, Dani Kerr, Erica Coffelt, Murmuration, Ryland Petty, Sharpe Family Singers, Steel Panther, and Three G.

As of this writing, dance group Murmuration has the most views for their latest performance on YouTube. They’re followed by the Sharpe Family Singers. The rest of the acts from Tuesday’s show currently have less than 100,000 views each.

However, online views isn’t a foolproof indicator for which acts America chose. This week also featured two Golden Buzzer acts — Murmuration and Atlanta Drum Academy. Could they be the two acts to make into the Finals?

To find out which acts made it through, be sure to watch the Qualifiers 2 results show on Wednesday night at 8/7c on NBC.
