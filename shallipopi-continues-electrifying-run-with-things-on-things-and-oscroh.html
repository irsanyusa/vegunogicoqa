Shallipopi continues electrifying run with 'Things on Things' &amp; 'Oscroh'

Publish date: 2024-08-07

These two new singles are the perfect follow-up to his highly successful debut EP, 'Planet Pluto,' and they showcase his musical evolution in spectacular fashion.

'Things on Things' is a vibrant composition that transports listeners into the heart of Afrobeats. Shallipopi's signature fusion of infectious rhythms and enchanting melodies takes center stage in this track.

The song encapsulates the essence of celebration, groove, and living life to the fullest. As the beat kicks in and Shallipopi's vocals weave their magic, it's impossible to resist the urge to move your feet.

One noteworthy highlight is the accompanying official video for "Things on Things," which elevates the song's energetic vibe.

With 'Oscroh (Pepperline),' Shallipopi takes a creative leap, showcasing his versatility as an artist. The track is a unique blend of flavors, infusing the unmistakable sounds of Afrobeats with a touch of spice.

The result is a dynamic and unforgettable musical journey that showcases Shallipopi's innovation and willingness to push boundaries.

Shallipopi's debut album, set to drop later this year, is already generating considerable buzz. With the success of 'Planet Pluto' and the excitement surrounding 'Things on Things' and 'Oscroh (Pepperline),' the album's arrival is eagerly anticipated by fans and music enthusiasts worldwide. It promises to be a defining moment for Shallipopi, and his audience is eager to join him on this musical adventure.
