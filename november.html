Publish date: 2024-08-20

Historical Events

Bowie's Last Performance

British rocker David Bowie performs on stage for the final time - three songs at a charity concert in New York City's Hammerstein Ballroom: "Wild Is the Wind", "Fantastic Voyage", and a duet of "Changes" with Alicia Keys

Famous Deaths

  • Ed Bradley, American journalist and CBS news correspondent (60 Minutes), dies of complications from lymphocytic leukemia at 65
  • Ellen Willis, American journalist and feminist, dies at 64
  • Markus Wolf, East German Intelligence Director, dies at 83
