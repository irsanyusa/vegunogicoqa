The Masked Singer has released a first-look teaser for Season 10, and it includes a preview of the theme nights we can expect to see. Host Nick Cannon also announces that this season will feature the “biggest costume to date.”

The Masked Singer Teases Season 10 in First Look

In the new teaser, panelist Jenny McCarthy calls Season 10 “the best season ever,” while Nicole Scherzinger can be heard telling a contestant, “You blew the roof off this place!” Ken Jeong calls this season “history-making.”

The panelists go on to tease that this season will feature the “Ding Dong, Keep It On” Bell, the Battle Royale, and the Smackdown. There will also be plenty of theme nights for fans to enjoy. This year’s themes include Trolls, Elton John, Harry Potter, Disco, 2000s, and Rock ‘n’ Roll.

McCarthy teases that “they went to the next level” with this season’s costumes. That includes the biggest costume in the show’s history, Cuddle Monster. McCarthy says this season also features “the biggest celebrity reveals yet.”

The clip also teases some past contestants who will return to the show this season for unmasked performances — including Jewel, aka Season 6 winner Queen of Hearts.

The New Season Premieres September 27

Earlier this month, The Masked Singer aired a Season 10 kickoff episode which teased some of this season’s contestants and featured a performance from Anonymouse, who was unmasked as singer Demi Lovato.

The official season premiere will be on Wednesday, September 27, on FOX. The longtime panel — Robin Thicke, Jenny McCarthy, Ken Jeong, and Nicole Scherzinger — will be back for the new competition, along with host Nick Cannon.

The show is also gearing up for Season 11, which is expected to premiere next spring. Filming is taking place this month, as Rita Ora steps in as a panelist. She’s replacing Nicole Scherzinger, who’s performing in London this fall.
