America’s Got Talent Season 16 contestant Peter Antoniou is set to join The Illusionists for their upcoming holiday shows. Antoniou’s fellow Season 16 contestant Léa Kyle will also be joining the group of touring magicians next year.

AGT Star Peter Antoniou Joins The Illusionists

Earlier this week, Antoniou shared the news on Instagram that he’ll be joining the cast of The Illusionists starting next week. He included a poster, on which he’s featured with several other magicians including Kevin James, Pablo Canovas, and Hyunjoon Kim.

The Illusionists is a touring magic show featuring a rotating cast of magicians representing different facets of the art of magic. The show premiered in Australia in 2012 and has toured the world, including shows on Broadway. The group is currently performing their Magic of the Holidays tour.

Antoniou will be part of the show on several upcoming dates in December, in cities around the United States such as Nashville, Detroit, Memphis, and New Orleans. You can purchase tickets to the show on the group’s website.

Fans might recognize Antoniou for his mentalist act, which took him to the Season 16 Semifinals. He also competed on AGT: All-Stars earlier this year.

Léa Kyle Is Joining the Show Next Year

Antoniou isn’t the only AGT Season 16 magician who is joining The Illusionists. Quick-change artist Léa Kyle, who reached the Finals after earning Heidi Klum’s Golden Buzzer, will be performing with the group next year.

Kyle and her fellow magicians (including her fiancé Florian Sainvet) will be performing in Paris at Folies Bergère beginning next February. The dates on the poster Kyle shared on Instagram run from February 21 to March 3, 2024.
