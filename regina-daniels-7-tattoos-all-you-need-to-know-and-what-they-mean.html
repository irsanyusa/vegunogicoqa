Regina Daniels 7 tattoos: All you need to know and what they mean

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Regina Daniels has expressed some of her styles through the art of tattoos and there are stories behind each of the six tattoos on her body.

The beautiful actress has picked up the tattoos in the last 10 years after being influenced by her brother, Sammy West.

Here's a list of her current inkings and the story behind each one.

The first word to be inked on Regina Daniels came as a phrase, ‘Forever 16’ with an infinity sign. The phrase with the sign tells how Daniels wants to feel for the rest of her life. The inking is situated just a little above her cleavage.

2. Heartbeat rate

Regina Daniels joined millions of tattoo lovers that have the Heartbeat tattoo. The tattoo is inked on her right wrist. The heartbeat tattoo is also referred to as the EKG line tattoo. The tattoo looks quite similar to the wavy lines that get formed as the heartbeats. There are diverse meanings associated with the tattoo such as remembrance of a loved one, or celebration of something special.

Just by the Nautical star inking on Regina Daniels’ right arm is the word Princess. If combined, it’s easier to say the actress refers to herself as the ‘Star Princess’.
