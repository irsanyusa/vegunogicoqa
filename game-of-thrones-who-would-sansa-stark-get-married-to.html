Who would Sansa Stark get married to?

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Another thing is who do you think she would end up marrying?

Sansa was officially married to Tyrion Lannister before she was given to Ramsay Bolton who raped and did all sort of mean things to her. The story is complicated, with Ramsay gone and Tyrion half way across to Westeros as the hand of the queen, we are left with Littlefinger.

Ramsay is dead, we are left with Littlefinger and Tyrion Lannister, till a new character pops up!

1. Tyrion Lannister

Well our inner minds tell us that Daenerys would win the war in Westeros, which automatically makes Tyrion Lannister the Hand of the Queen for the Seven Kingdoms. He needs a bride.

Did he ever love Sansa? Or does he want to stay married to Sansa [they are officially married]?

We believe the duo [Sansa and Tyrion] were starting to like each other before the death of Jeoffrey separated them. This means they are still legally married!

2. Littlefinger

You all would agree that Littlefinger is the King in the game of chess. Though he tells people not to trust him [which they end up doing] he doesn't look like the culprit but always ignites the fire of war between his enemies.

He was in love with Sansa's mom and has professed his love for Sansa several times, but we are not certain they would end up together.

Who do you think she would end up with? Tyrion Lannister, the hand of the Queen or Littlefinger, the King of the chess game.
