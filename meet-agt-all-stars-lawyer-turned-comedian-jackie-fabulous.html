Meet 'AGT All-Stars' Lawyer-Turned-Comedian Jackie Fabulous

Publish date: 2024-09-04

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent: All-Stars

Casey Durkin/NBC

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Jackie Fabulous took America’s Got Talent season 14 stage by storm when she delivered a hilarious stand-up routine during her audition. She didn’t reach the Finale of the show, but she’s now back for redemption on AGT All-Stars. Here’s everything to know about her.

Jackie Fabulous Earned a Law Degree Before She Pursued Comedy

Originally from The Bronx, New York, Jackie Fabulous moved to Los Angeles, California at the age of 29, to study law. According to the comedian, her Law School Admission Test (LSAT) scores were “horrible,” so she entered whichever school would willingly take her. She later stayed in Trinity Law School in LA, finishing her four years there.

As she was working her way to a law school diploma, Jackie eloped with her then-boyfriend who she divorced from a year later. Since then, she’s worked multiple day jobs to survive. After a series of existential crisis, Jackie has finally discovered where her heart finds its purpose — comedy. This major career shift has probably become her best decision so far. Having endured her fair share of loves, tragedies, and embarrassing moments, Jackie channels such energy into her stand-up routines.

“She is so comedically versatile, she could work a bar mitzvah, a bachelorette party, or your uncle’s funeral,” her website bio reads.

Jackie’s “smart sense of fun” has made her a relatable icon in comedy. Through the years, she has become a multifaceted performer, attracting audience of all races, genders, and socio-economic classes.

She Made Simon Cowell Blush After Her Audition

In 2019, she joined AGT and tickled America’s funny bone in her every performance. Her audition consisted of making jokes about her mother telling her she’s fat, sharing her opinion on weight loss competitions and finding friends on Facebook. Jackie got four yeses from the judges. But before Simon Cowell gave her his nod, he walked on stage to hug her.

She continued to do comedy after the competition, hosting and getting featured in television shows. Jackie has performed stan-up routines in The Arsenio Hall show, Gotham Comedy Live, Last Comic Standing, and Kevin Harts LOL Network.

In addition to these, she’s become a paid regular and headlines in top comedy clubs including The Comedy Cellar and The Hollywood Improv. She’s also performed in The Laugh Factory, The Comedy and Magic Club, and the House of Comedy.

Jackie Released Her Stand-Up Special Menoplause

As a body-positive Black woman, she intends to share her perspective about dating and sex, menopause and female friendships. This was made possible through Menoplause, her newest stand-up special. Today, it’s available in various video-streaming platforms.

Aside from this creative project, Jackie entertains her social media followers by posting jokes on Instagram and Twitter. Last November, she also announced the fabulous news that she’ll be competing in AGT spin-off series, AGT All-Stars.
