The Normal People Soundtrack Is Your Next Favorite Spotify Playlist

Publish date: 2024-05-18

And capturing the tone of Rooney's novel ( a literary sensation both in Rooney's native Ireland, where the novel is set, and on this side of the pond) was no easy task.

Normal People reads like Jane Austen but with texting instead of snail mail (and that's not meant to be a dig; quite the opposite). It's a novel with such attention to its character's confused and hormonal thoughts, that a cinematic adaptation (without such internal attention) might just look, outwardly, like soft-core Oxygen network porn.

But somehow it's not softcore Oxygen porn. In fact, the series, aided no doubt by the involvement of author Sally Rooney, manages to not only convey literarily-rich character (and without showing texts or hearing thoughts), but the series also captures perfectly the tone of the novel, something like: euphoric melancholia. Not easy.
