Kristin Chenoweth Archives - Talent Recap

Publish date: 2024-09-15

FULL NAME: Kristi Dawn Chenoweth

HOMETOWN: Broken Arrow, Oklahoma

BIRTHDAY: July 24, 1968

AGE: 56

RELATIONSHIP STATUS: Married to Josh Bryant

BIO: Kristin Chenoweth is notoriously known as the originator of “Glinda” in the hit Broadway musical, Wicked. Ahead of her performance as Glinda, Chenoweth broke into Broadway in the show Steel Pier. She later went on to appear in You’re a Good Man, Charlie Brown, and Wicked, soon after. Chenoweth has also been featured in various TV shows and films like her own sitcom Kristin, Sesame Street, Glee, Bewitched, Pink Panther, and more. In 2021, Chenoweth acted as a mentor to Team Ariana, while Ariana Grande was a coach on The Voice.
