What Happened on March 26, 1926

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Historical Events

  • 85th Grand National: Billy Watkinson victorious aboard 25/1 bet Jack Horner; first Australian jockey to win the race
  • ACD de Graeff appointed governor general of Dutch East Indies
  • First lip-reading tournament held in America

Famous Birthdays

  • Charles Champlin, American film critic and writer (Los Angeles Times, 1965-80), television host, and educator, born in Hammondsport, New York (d. 2014)

Famous Deaths

  • Konstantin Fehrenbach, German Chancellor of the Weimar Republic(1920-21), dies at 74
