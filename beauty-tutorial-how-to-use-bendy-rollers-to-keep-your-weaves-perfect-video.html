How to use bendy rollers to keep your weaves perfect [Video]

Publish date: 2024-09-02

In essence weaves especially do not need curling wands/iron/straighteners everytime as most times the heatless bendy rollers can do all trick,leaving the curls tight or loose or in any way they are desired.

Bendy rollers do not get enough credit, they are featured as bendy foam hair rollers that can be used without heat- which gives it an edge as heat shouldn't be applied to weaves and hair at the barest minimum.

The great thing about the bendy rollers is the fact that they can be used on wet or dry hair to get ringlet curls, bouncy curls, loose curls and so on without just a little turn and bend here and there, they are absolutely easy to use and they pose no harm at all.
