Rapper opens up on beef with Chris Brown over Karrueche Tran

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Speaking on the Breakfast club, he said:

"All I did, I saw some pictures of Karrueche that were cool and I double tapped them, like I usually do. Everybody took it all out of context. I didn't really care about how it would make anybody feel, I just double tapped some pictures. I was actually thinking about how I would feel and I thought the pictures were amazing so I double tapped em."

Speaking on what he looks for in a woman, he says,

"As far as women go you have to be damn near amazing for me to take you seriously. Most women that I come across these days, in hip hop and what not, aren't really worth the time. All the real women are in the workplace but I mean you can't just walk in a building and be like, 'yeah it's you', you know what I mean? So you have to wait until you meet somebody by chance or something like that, and you know that gets old so you end up fingering in the park."

Hopefully, this would put an end to the beef, just as far as Chris Breezy buys the story.
