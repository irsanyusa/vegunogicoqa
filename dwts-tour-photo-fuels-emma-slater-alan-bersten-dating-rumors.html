A new photo of Emma Slater and Alan Bersten has Dancing with the Stars fans talking. The shot, taken during rehearsals for the show’s upcoming tour, is fueling rumors of a romance between the two pro dancers.

DWTS Tour Photo Fuels Emma Slater, Alan Bersten Rumors

On Friday, the official Instagram account for the DWTS tour posted a collection of behind-the-scenes photos from rehearsals. One of the images shows Slater and Bersten sitting next to each other and smiling. Slater is touching Bersten’s hand.

The photo only added fuel to the rumors that the two dancers are romantically involved. Fans on Reddit shared their differing opinions about the idea that they’re dating, with one user writing of the photo, “Lol and yet you all refuse to believe the ‘rumors’ about them.”

Another user commented on the fact that the official tour account shared the photo, writing, “I can’t believe they would post this lol.” Someone else referred to the rumors that Slater and Bersten were together during her marriage, writing, “Side piece moving up to boyfriend status after 4 years of waiting. You go guys!”

Sasha Farber Fueled Rumors Last Month

Slater filed for divorce from her fellow pro Sasha Farber earlier this year. Last month, Farber fueled rumors of a romance between Slater and Bersten by liking a tweet hinting at a relationship between the two dancers.

The tweet in question referred to a blind item on the gossip account Deux Moi claiming that two DWTS pros who “have been rumored to have been on and off for years (while one party was married)” went on a double date with “another pro and her previous partner turned boyfriend.”

Some fans deduced that the blind item might be about Slater and Bersten, with the other couple being pro Britt Stewart and her boyfriend Daniel Durant. However, other fans aren’t amused by the constant speculation about Slater’s love life.

One Reddit user wrote that Slater is “linked to any man she’s breathing air next to.” That includes her former DWTS partners Trevor Donovan and Mauricio Umansky, as well as fellow pro Gleb Savchenko.
