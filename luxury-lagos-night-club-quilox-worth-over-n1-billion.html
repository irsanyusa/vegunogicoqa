Quilox, Worth Over N1 Billion

Publish date: 2024-09-11

The club’s chairman Shina Peller revealed this, during a business brunch with journalists in Lagos, ahead of the club’s re-opening, last Friday.

The estimate covers the cost of the property, the internal club structure, fittings and assets. Peller also said the club’s value also includes identified brand equity.

It took over two years to construct the luxury nightclub, which is located on Ozumba Mbadiwe road, in Victoria Island, Lagos.

Quilox sits on multiple plots on the super premium Ozumba Mbadiwe with its impeccable design and finishing complemented with stunning and first of its kind décor and lighting, world class customer service and hospitality, renowned DJs and an over the roof sound system that pundits say is the first in this market.

Quilox, which re-opened on the 1 of August 2014 after a brief hiatus is now optimized for even more posh partying, with the introduction of the VVIP Friday night gigs, as well as karaoke and the innovative Quilox pocket money.
