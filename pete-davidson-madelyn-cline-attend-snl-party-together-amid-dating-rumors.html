Rumored couple Pete Davidson and Madelyn Cline were spotted together over the weekend attending a Saturday Night Live afterparty together. Davidson hosted Saturday’s Season 49 premiere of the show.

Pete Davidson, Madelyn Cline Attend SNL Afterparty

According to People, Cline accompanied Davidson to the official SNL afterparty on Saturday night at Catch Steak in New York City. Davidson, who used to be a cast member on the comedy show, made his hosting debut this weekend.

“They seemed very lovey, very cute, but they kept to themselves,” a source said of the pair’s behavior at the party, adding that they were “not very social.” The source shared, “[Davidson] stayed pretty late. He was there till after 4 a.m.”

A few weeks ago, Cline was seen attending Davidson’s stand-up gig in Las Vegas. Although they haven’t officially confirmed their relationship, sources broke the news of their romance last month. Davidson broke up with actress Chase Sui Wonders in August.

Cline previously dated her Outer Banks co-star Chase Stokes. They split in November 2021. Stokes is currently dating country star Kelsea Ballerini.

Taylor Swift, Travis Kelce Also Attended the Party

Davidson and Cline weren’t the only rumored couple attending the SNL afterparty over the weekend. Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce also stepped out for the event. During the show, Kelce made a cameo in a sketch poking fun at their romance, while Swift introduced musical guest Ice Spice.

Swift and Kelce were photographed holding hands while attending the afterparty. American Idol judge Katy Perry showed her support for the pairing by commenting on the photos, “I ship.”

Rumors have been swirling about Swift and Kelce’s relationship status for weeks now, as the pop star was spotted attending a few Kansas City Chiefs games. If their romance wasn’t already obvious, this weekend’s PDA certainly seems like a confirmation that the two are dating.
